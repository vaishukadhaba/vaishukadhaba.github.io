Surti Petis Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 English muffins
  • 4 eggs
  • 8 slices of cooked ham
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. To make the hollandaise sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan over low heat. In a blender, combine the egg yolks, lemon juice, and cayenne pepper. Blend for a few seconds to combine.
  2. With the blender running, slowly pour the melted butter into the blender in a thin stream until the sauce is thick and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. To poach the eggs, fill a large saucepan with about 3 inches of water and add the vinegar. Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
  4. Crack one egg into a small bowl. Using a spoon, create a whirlpool in the simmering water and drop the egg into the center of the whirlpool. Repeat with the remaining eggs, cooking them one or two at a time.
  5. Cook the eggs for about 3-4 minutes, until the whites are set and the yolks are still runny.
  6. While the eggs are cooking, toast the English muffins and warm the slices of ham in a frying pan.
  7. To assemble, place a slice of ham on each toasted English muffin half. Using a slotted spoon, carefully remove the poached eggs from the water and place one on each muffin half. Spoon hollandaise sauce over the eggs and serve immediately.
